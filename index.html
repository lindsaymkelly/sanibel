Sanibel Island

Welcome to my site dedicated to one of my favorite places in the world, Sanibel Island!

Facts About Sanibel:

  • Sanibel is located off the west coast of Florida, close to Fort Myers.
  • The island has an estimated population of less than 7,000 people.
  • The island has beautiful beaches and wildlife reserves.
  • One of the most popular activities on the island is shelling, as the beaches have a very high quantity of shells.
